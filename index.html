Kids, A Limmerick

For those times when you don't understand anything at all but you're still able to make it better.

My girls like to cry when the're sad
My girls like to cry when they're mad
I just don't understand
Emotions they command
But I love to hear them call for Dad.

My personal interests

My big interest right now is options trading. I love numbers, charts and money. I enjoy trying to find under aprreciated companies that have a great product but for some reason the 'experts' haven't noticed them yet.

I love camping!! For entirely too long I was not able to go camping and spend much time outside. This summer the plan is to go at least once a month. My wife an I got tents (one for us, one for the kids), sleeping bags, air matresses, space heaters, propane grills, etc. I guess camping has now turned into glamping, but the older we get, the more comfortable we want to be.

Another interest is wood working. I took a class a few years ago and spent a lot of time making cabinets, tables, hope chests, and picture frames. We also made a few storage sheds which turned out to be really small homes. I learned that I really enjoy working with wood, turning it into something useful and functional.
